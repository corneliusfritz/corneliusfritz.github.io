An implementation of generalized linear models (GLMs) for studying
relationships among attributes in connected populations, where responses
of connected units can be dependent, as introduced by Fritz et al.
(2025)
\<[doi:10.1080/01621459.2025.2565851](https://doi.org/10.1080/01621459.2025.2565851)\>.
'igml' extends GLMs for independent responses to dependent responses and
can be used for studying spillover in connected populations and other
network-mediated phenomena.
